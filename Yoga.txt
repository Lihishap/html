<h1> Yoga World </h1>
<p>Originally from India, YOGA is a discipline that includes body postures (asanas), breathing control exercises (pranayamas) and meditation exercises.

In Sanskrit, a traditional ancient Indian language, the word “YOGA” means “UNIFY” and symbolizes the union of body and mind.
